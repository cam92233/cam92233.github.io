Tips on how to pay off student loans:

  • If you ever get a lump sum of cash (holidays, birthday, graduation, etc.), always set aside some of that money towards the loans!
  • Try modifying your current budget to accomodate increased loan payments (Ex. Instead of $400 a month on groceries/going out food, lower it to just groceries at $300 a month)
  • ALWAYS pay off more than the required minimum payment each month
  • Maybe start a little side job for extra cash (Ex. Uber on the weekends)
  • If you are wanting to pay off these loans faster, stay motivated and stick with a plan!
